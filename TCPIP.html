   I had to write a paper about a freely chosen topic for my finals in high school. I had upgraded my home network, and had some fresh knowledge about networking I wanted to share, so I chose the TCP/IP Model.

   I also had to give a talk about it at my finals, so if you need somebody to talk about basic networking and have a seven minute limitation - I'm really well prepared to explain the basics of internetworking in a very short amount of time.
   If you are just interested in a short introduction about the TCP/IP model, including a overview of the history of the internet and the OSI model, and understand basic german, you can download my paper here.
